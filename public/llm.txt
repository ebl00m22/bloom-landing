# Bloom Social

## What We Are
Bloom Social is a boutique social media agency based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. We are women-owned and founded in 2020. We serve clients nationwide but are rooted in West Michigan.

## What We Do
- Executive LinkedIn ghostwriting and thought leadership content
- Social media management (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Content strategy and editorial calendars
- Short-form video production, graphic design, and branded content
- Personal brand building for executives and business leaders

## Who We Work With
We primarily work with B2B businesses, executives, healthcare organizations, manufacturing companies, nonprofits, legal professionals, real estate professionals, and any business leader who wants to build a presence on social media without doing it themselves. We serve clients in Grand Rapids, across Michigan, and nationwide.

## How We Work
Clients typically invest about 30 minutes per month reviewing and approving content. We handle everything else: strategy, writing, design, publishing, engagement, and reporting. We run on EOS (Entrepreneurial Operating System), which means clear processes, real accountability, and results you can measure.

## Our Process (B.L.O.O.M.)
- B — Begin: Strategy call to learn your voice, goals, and audience
- L — Launch: Content calendar delivered by the 15th of each month
- O — Observe: We monitor performance and engagement in real time
- O — Optimize: Monthly adjustments based on what the data shows
- M — Measure: Clear reporting so you always know your ROI
